Telvin Smith Bio: Age, Brother, Height

NFL Career Stats

Telvin Smith took his first part in the National Football League as the fifth-round pick of the Jacksonville Jaguars at the 2014 NFL Draft. He landed his professional regular season debut for the Jaguars in a season-opener 34-17 loss against the Philadelphia Eagles. Nevertheless, Telvin marked some impression with three combined tackles over the course of the game.

Later on 11 October 2015, he recorded a season-high 14 combined tackles during his side’s 38-21 loss against the Tampa Bay Buccaneers. Telvin appeared in all 16 games in 2018 and recorded a career-high 134 combined tackles, two deflections, two interceptions, and one touchdown.

Contract, Salary
