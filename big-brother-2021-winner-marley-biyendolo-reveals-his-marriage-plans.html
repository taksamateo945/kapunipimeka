Big Brother 2021 winner Marley Biyendolo reveals his marriage plans

June 2024 · 2 minute read

Big Brother winner Marley Biyendolo has revealed his marriage plans with his U.S.-based girlfriend Lynn Williams. 

The basketballer, 26, told The Daily Telegraph on Thursday a trip down the aisle was on the cards for the long-distance couple.

‘That is the plan for our future,’ he explained to the publication.  

Tying the knot: Big Brother winner Marley Biyendolo has revealed his marriage plans with American soccer player girlfriend Lynn Williams

‘She will play soccer for another three years [in America] and at the end of all of that, we would love to settle down here in Australia,’ he added.

‘So marriage is definitely in the works and in our plans for the future.’

Lynn will play for the United States soccer team at the Tokyo Olympics. 

Settling down: ‘She will play soccer for another three years [in America] and at the end of all of that, we would love to settle down here in Australia,’ Marley told The Daily Telegraph, adding that marriage ‘is the plan for our future’

The pair met while studying at Pepperdine University near Malibu, California.

They have been physically separated for 18 months due to the Covid pandemic, but remain committed to each other.

‘It is brutal. Corona has messed up a lot of people but I am thankful and I have to look on the brighter side of things,’ he said.

Struggles: The pair met while studying in California. They have been physically separated for 18 months due to the Covid pandemic, but remain committed to each other

‘It is hard that I haven’t obviously seen her but we will be together soon. Thank God for technology,’ he added. 

Marley was crowned the winner of Big Brother during Tuesday night’s live finale, after spending 63 days in the house.

Marley received the most public votes, ahead of jewellery designer Sarah Jane ‘SJ’ Adams and flight attendant Christina Podolyan.
