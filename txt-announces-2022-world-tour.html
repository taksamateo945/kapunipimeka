TXT Announces 2022 World Tour

June 2024 · 2 minute read

TXT, a K-pop group, has announced a world tour. The world-famous K-pop boy group has finally announced its first-ever 2022 TOMORROW X TOGETHER WORLD TOUR ACT: LOVE SICK. BIGHIT MUSIC, the boy band’s management company, announced the exciting news on social media with an intriguing dark poster.

In addition, the group is preparing to release its fourth mini-album, minisode 2:Thursday’s Child, on May 9 at 6 p.m. KST. The Chaos Chapter: Fight Or Escape, a repackaged version of their sophomore album The Chaos Chapter: Freeze, was released nine months ago.

Dates for TXT’s world tour in 2022, as well as other information

BIGHIT MUSIC, the K-pop boy group’s agency, took to Twitter on April 26, 2022, to announce the quintet’s upcoming world tour, ACT: LOVESICK, which will begin in Seoul in July. The agency has currently confirmed nine shows in South Korea and the United States.

According to the group’s recently updated new tour page on BIGHIT MUSIC’s official website, the quintet’s upcoming 2022 world tour will include “more to come.” As of now, it is unclear whether this additional information indicates additional dates or venue details.

The most recent confirmed dates for the band’s upcoming world tour in

July 2022 are as follows:

  • 2 – Seoul, South Korea
  • 3 – Seoul, South Korea
  • 7 – Chicago, US
  • 9 – New York, US
  • 12 – Atlanta, US
  • 14 – Dallas, US
  • 17 – Houston, US
  • 21 – San Francisco, US
  • 23 – Los Angeles, US

The group’s agency will release more information about concert tickets and venues in the coming days. Those interested should keep an eye on BIGHIT MUSIC’s official website for the most up-to-date information on the group’s upcoming concerts.

Fans react to TXT’s announcement of a world tour in 2022.

Fans took to social media shortly after hearing the unexpected news to express their excitement about the quintet’s upcoming 2022 world tour. They also want the agency to add more tour dates in various cities and countries.
