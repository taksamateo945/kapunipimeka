About

“Les Miserables” is a popular musical based on the 1862 novel of the same name by Victor Hugo. The musical features a large cast, including lead characters such as:

  • Jean Valjean: a former convict who becomes an honest man and adopts the orphaned Cosette
  • Inspector Javert: a police inspector who spends years hunting down Valjean
  • Fantine: a factory worker who turns to prostitution to provide for her daughter, Cosette
  • Cosette: Fantine’s daughter, whom Valjean rescues from the Thénardiers and raises as his own
  • Marius Pontmercy: a young student who falls in love with Cosette

The original London production of “Les Miserables” premiered in 1985, while the Broadway production opened in 1987. The musical has since been performed in many countries around the world and has been adapted into several film versions.

Over the years, numerous actors have played these iconic roles in stage productions and film adaptations. Some notable performers include Hugh Jackman as Jean Valjean in the 2012 film adaptation, Anne Hathaway as Fantine in the same film, Colm Wilkinson as the original Jean Valjean in both the London and Broadway productions, and Lea Salonga as Eponine in the 10th Anniversary Concert production.
